Masala Omelette

A protein-rich omelette flavored with Indian spices.

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Green chilli
  • Salt
  • Pepper
Cooking Steps
  1. Beat eggs with spices.
  2. Add chopped onion and chilli.
  3. Pour into heated pan.
  4. Cook until done.
